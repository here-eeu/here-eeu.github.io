4. Publish ArcGIS/QGIS project:

For this workshop I've chosen open data from the geofabrik web-site. And made a couple operations to prepare datasets and convert them to GeoJson format.

Let's download and extract archive with the data Download

vilnus_admin_boundary.geojson – administrative boundary of Vilnius with a polygon geometry

vilnius_railway.geojson – railways layer of Vilnius with a linear geometry

vilnius_roads.geojson – roads layer of Vilnius with a linear geometry

vilnius_stations.geojson – bus, bike, train stations of Vilnius with a point geometry

Open XYZ Studio and let's have a look on Data Hub:

Then open Projects and Create new Project. Give it a name:

Add data from Data Hub using Add+ button:

Select layer from the list and click Add dataset:

Now you can change the style of the layer, change cards to display and a lot of other things to customize visualization:

We have different stations in vilnius_stations.geojson and it would be better to style features based on the fclass column. There are five possible values:

  • bus_station
  • bus_stop
  • railway_station
  • railway_halt
  • taxi
